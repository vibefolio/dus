## üõ†Ô∏è Improvement Checklist (10 Points per Template)

1.  **Unique Visual Concept**: Distinct color palette, fonts, and layout.
2.  **Mobile Optimization**: Perfect hamburger menu and touch interactions.
3.  **Hero Section Impact**: Video backgrounds, scroll animations, or bold typography.
4.  **Core Feature - Booking/Browsing**: Functional reservation modal or product view.
5.  **Micro-Interactions**: Hover effects, button animations, scroll reveals.
6.  **Navigation System**: Sticky headers, transparent-to-solid transitions.
7.  **Content Authenticity**: High-quality images (no placeholders) and realistic copy.
8.  **SEO Structure**: Semantic HTML, proper H1-H6 hierarchy.
9.  **Loading Experience**: Smooth fade-ins or skeleton screens.
10. **Information Architecture**: Clear sections (About, Service, Reviews, Contact).
